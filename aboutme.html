About Me

I’m a UX Designer crafting responsive, functional design solutions for complex issues.

My journey into UX Design begins with the fusion of two fields of interest: art and technology. As a digital artist and Information Systems graduate from Baylor University, I had two passions I kept debating over. Which one would I really do? The answer: both!

In December 2016, I applied for taking the designer track at Bloc––an online bootcamp--and got started on my studies. I could finally learn how to link my two passions into one.

What I learned in college inspired me to start UX Design. I had some exposure to UI design, information architecture, graphics design, and programming (Visual Basic and C#) in a few of my major courses. These experiences fueled my interest in studying design. In addition, helped me complete my design program.

My overall journey was like being an adventurer in a fictional tale. I had to overcome many obstacles, mistakes, and errors that made me stronger and better overall both in life and as a designer. The journey is never ending but enjoyable every step of the way.

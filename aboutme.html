Thank you and Welcome!

Hello again! Thank you for taking the time to get to know more about me. Let's get started~

I'm a user experience designer from Houston, TX. I love learning; trying new things; teaching martial arts; and helping myself to a hot cup of mocha. I design landing pages; mobile and web apps; and I can draw awesome girls.

My journey into UX Design begins with the fusion of two fields of interest: art and technology. As a digital artist and Information Systems graduate from Baylor University, I had two passions I kept debating over. Which one would I really do? The answer: both!

My Journey

In December 2016, I applied for taking the designer track at Bloc––an online bootcamp--and got started on my studies. This was decided after spending the Summer of 2016 trying to figure out how I could link my two passions together. Thanks to UX Design and Bloc, I found my answer.

What I learned in college inspired me to start UX Design. I had some exposure to UI design, Information Architecture, Graphics Design, and programming (Visual Basic and C#) in a few of my major courses with my group projects. These experiences fueled my interest in studying UX Design. In addition, helped me complete my design program.

My overall journey was like being an adventurer in a fictional tale. I had to overcome many obstacles; mistakes; and errors that made me stronger and better overall both in life and as a designer. The journey is never ending but enjoyable every step of the way.
